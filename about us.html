About Us

Newer Earth aims to educate and inform homeowners who have overlooked the natural environment, surrounding sustainable and eco-friendly practices. We encourage these homeowners to take greater responsibility for their local environment.

Through this initiative, Newer Earth promotes sustainable living and offers a platform where individuals can learn how to protect and nurture their communities.

We encourage community through conservation, making sustainability enjoyable, practical, and inclusive for everyone.

How We Work

We build community; we're about people as much as about the environment.

We inspire individuals to make a positive impact on the environment while benefiting themselves and enjoying the process, all through the most eco-friendly methods available.

We bring conservation into your home through virtual experiences, reaching individuals who may have never had access to environmental education in high school.
